‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with the API?
First, ensure you have a Business plan. Then check the API documentation and start with a simple test request to /api/generate. Our team can provide personalized onboarding if needed.
What are the rate limits for API requests?
Business plan users get 100 requests per minute and 1000 videos per day. Batch processing supports up to 6 videos per request. Contact us if you need higher limits.
How long does video processing take?
Processing time varies by video length and complexity. Typical clips take 2-5 minutes, while longer videos may take up to 10 minutes. Use the /api/progress endpoint to track status.
Can I customize the output video specifications?
Business plan users automatically get High Quality Processing output in 9:16 portrait format. Custom resolutions and formats are available through our enterprise solutions.
How do I handle API errors and timeouts?
Implement exponential backoff for rate limit errors (429), validate YouTube URLs before sending, and use longer timeouts for video processing requests. Check our error handling guide for details.
